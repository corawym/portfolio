What is Smart Select

Smart Select is dedicated to serve the community on another level. It seeks to provide solid information to ensure that consumers make the most informed and best choices. In addition, Smart Select tells readers and consumers “what” is trending and requires attention, “why” it matters to them, “how” it will affect them, “where” it is happening, and “who” they should go to. At its launch, Smart Select is the only content-driven Chinese consumer magazine in the Greater Vancouver.
