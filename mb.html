Process

mb. My Book is a quarterly magazine, from fashion, food, travel tips to local activities. I solved complicated publication layout issues, such as balancing advertisements with content. I collaborated with editors, marketing manager and advertising manager in order to discuss the appearance of the upcoming issue. Besides, I designed visual layout of a magazine by using photos, graphics and other forms of artwork to create the covers and all inside pages.
